Agno shipped eleven releases in June 2026, significantly expanding the framework's capabilities. Major additions included four new model providers, Learnings CRUD functionality on AgentOS, checkpointing and run forking features, and new toolkits for search and file generation. The month also brought improvements to observability, security, and community integrations.
